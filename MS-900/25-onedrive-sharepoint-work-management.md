# Role of OneDrive and SharePoint in Work Management

OneDrive and SharePoint are essential storage and collaboration services in Microsoft 365.

---

## OneDrive for Business

- Personal cloud storage for employees
- Easy file sharing with colleagues
- Access files from any device
- Version history and file recovery

---

## SharePoint Online

- Team and organization-wide document libraries
- Centralized content management
- Permissions and access control
- Integration with Teams and Planner

---

## How They Support Work Management

- Store project files centrally
- Enable document collaboration
- Ensure secure and organized file access
- Support team productivity

OneDrive and SharePoint form the backbone of document management in Microsoft 365.
