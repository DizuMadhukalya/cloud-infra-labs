# Responding to Threats in Defender for Office 365

## Zero-hour Auto Purge (ZAP)
Removes malicious emails after delivery.

## Automated Investigation and Response (AIR)
Automatically remediates threats.

## SIEM Integration
Logs forwarded to Microsoft Sentinel for correlation.

---

## Real-Life Example

Malicious email initially bypasses filters.
ZAP removes it from all mailboxes within minutes.
