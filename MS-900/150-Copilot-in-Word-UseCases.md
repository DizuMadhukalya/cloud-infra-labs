\# Microsoft Copilot in Word — Practical Use Cases



Copilot in Word assists users in drafting, rewriting, summarizing, and generating documents using natural language prompts.



\## Capabilities

\- Draft documents from prompts

\- Rewrite content professionally

\- Summarize long reports

\- Generate proposals and letters



\## Use Case

HR drafts policy documents using prompts like “Create a leave policy for employees”.



\## Example

User pastes raw notes and asks Copilot to convert them into a structured report.
