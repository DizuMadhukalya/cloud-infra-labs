The Home tab in Viva Insights gives users a personalized starting point to understand their work patterns and growth.

## Sections Included

### Recommended for You
Personalized suggestions based on work habits such as scheduling focus time, following up on tasks, and reducing meeting overload.

### Your Progress
Tracks wellbeing and productivity goals over time such as focus hours, quiet hours, and collaboration patterns.

### Inspiration Library
Provides guided practices, tips, and short learning content to improve productivity and mental wellbeing.

## Use Case
An employee sees a recommendation to block focus time after noticing too many meetings in the week.

## Example
The dashboard shows the user completed 3 focus sessions this week compared to last week's 1 session.
