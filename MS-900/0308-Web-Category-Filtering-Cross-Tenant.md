# Web-Category Filtering in Cross-Tenant Access

Web-category filtering allows administrators to restrict access to categories of websites.

Categories may include:
- Social Media
- Gambling
- Malware
- File Sharing

---

## Why Important in Cross-Tenant

External users may attempt to access SaaS apps from risky domains.

---

## Real-Life Example

Vendor accessing internal portal:
- Allowed corporate apps
- Blocked access from anonymous proxy websites

This reduces risk exposure.
