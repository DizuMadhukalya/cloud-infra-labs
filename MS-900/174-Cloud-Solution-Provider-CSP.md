# Cloud Solution Provider (CSP)

The Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) program allows organizations to purchase Microsoft 365 licenses through Microsoft partners instead of buying directly from Microsoft.

## Key Characteristics
- Purchased via Microsoft partners
- Flexible monthly or annual billing
- Partner provides support and billing management
- Suitable for small and medium organizations

## Use Case
A small business buys Microsoft 365 Business Premium through a local IT partner who manages billing and support.

## Example
Partner provisions licenses, handles renewals, and provides first-line support.
