# Defender for Office 365 in the Microsoft Defender Portal

The Microsoft Defender portal (security.microsoft.com) provides a centralized interface to manage, investigate, and respond to email threats.

## Key Sections

### Investigations
Shows ongoing and completed automated investigations triggered by suspicious emails.

Example:
A phishing campaign targeting finance employees triggers automated investigation and removes malicious emails.

---

### Explorer (Threat Explorer)
Allows security teams to search and analyze threats across emails, URLs, attachments, and users.

Example:
Security analyst searches for emails containing suspicious link across organization.

---

### Review Section
Provides quarantine, user-reported emails, and pending security actions.

Example:
Admin reviews quarantined email and releases legitimate email.

---

### Campaigns
Identifies coordinated email attack campaigns.

Example:
Defender identifies global phishing campaign targeting banking sector.

---

### Threat Tracker
Provides intelligence about active threats worldwide.

Example:
Admin learns about new ransomware campaign targeting Office 365 users.

---

### Exchange Message Trace
Tracks email delivery path.

Example:
Admin traces why email was delayed or blocked.

---

### Attack Simulation Training
Simulates phishing attacks to train employees.

Example:
HR conducts phishing simulation to improve employee awareness.

---

### Policies and Rules
Configure anti-spam, anti-phishing, and safe attachment policies.
