# Commit 369 - Device and Software Inventories in MDVM

## Overview

Inventories provide a complete list of:

- Devices
- Installed software
- Operating systems

---

## Device Inventory

Shows:

- Device name
- OS version
- Risk level

Example:

Detect outdated Windows 10 system.

---

## Software Inventory

Shows installed applications.

Example:

Detect vulnerable Adobe Reader.

---

## Real-life Use Case

Company discovers unauthorized software installed by employee.

---

## Benefits

- Full visibility
- Risk identification

---

## Conclusion

Inventories help manage security across devices.
