# Meetings in Microsoft Teams

## Types of Meetings
- Online meetings
- Webinars
- Live Events
- Teams Phone

## Features
- Screen sharing and recording
- Breakout rooms
- Meeting chat
- Copilot summaries
- PSTN calling

## Use Case
Webinar hosted with registration, Q&A, and recording shared via Stream.

## Example
Copilot generates summary, action items, and follow-ups after meetings.
