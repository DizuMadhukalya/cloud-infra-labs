# Management of the Cloud

Management of the cloud refers to how cloud providers manage and maintain the underlying infrastructure.

---

## What is Management of the Cloud?

It includes:

- Hardware maintenance  
- Data center operations  
- Security patching  
- Network management  

These tasks are handled by Microsoft Azure.

---

## Benefits

### 1. No Infrastructure Maintenance

Users do not need to worry about:

- Physical servers  
- Power and cooling  
- Hardware failures  

---

### 2. Automatic Updates

Azure manages:

- OS patches  
- Firmware updates  
- Platform improvements  

---

### 3. High Reliability

Cloud providers ensure:

- Redundant systems  
- Disaster recovery  
- Global data centers  

---

## Conclusion

Management of the cloud removes the burden of infrastructure management from customers and allows them to focus on applications and business needs.
